About

Allow me to introduce myself.

Hi! I'm Cassie, a creative developer with a passion for creating interactive designs that allow users to engage with various products. I’m a graduate from the University of California Davis majoring in exercise biology. While attending UC Davis I participated in Camp Adventure Youth Services, a program designed to provide childcare support to US and allied forces military families located abroad. I provided dynamic youth camps on multiple bases throughout Europe and later became a training manager. I took the experience gained through my work with Camp Adventure and began a career in child development; working as a Director at the Teen Centre on Supreme Allied Headquarters Europe (NATO) base in Mons, Belgium. My time as Director of the Teen Centre bred in me an interest in design. I was responsible for creating advertising for upcoming events, and as such developed a passion for web design. I’m currently gaining experience working through the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp. I enjoy creating interactive designs that allow users to engage with various products. Upon completion of my course I will be pursuing a position as a front end developer, allowing me to create dynamic designs that interface directly with the user.
